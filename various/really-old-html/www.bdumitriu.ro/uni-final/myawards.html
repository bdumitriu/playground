The best result I achieved in the 11th grade at the Computer Science "olympics", where I placed third in regional phase. These "olympics" are the most prestigious contests in Romania for high school students. They consist of four phases: a school phase, a city phase, a regional phase and a national phase. They occur once a year for each major high school subject.
